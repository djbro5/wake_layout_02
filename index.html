CONSTRUCTION

CONSTRUCTION

The Wake Construction Group Construction Division specialise in Building Management, Project Management, Construction Techniques and Project Estimation.

Our continuous improvement strategy covers all aspects of project delivery, in particular, the application of modern technology to construction systems, techniques and materials. Example is our use of Cross Laminated Panels as a modern construction material - see Carpentry for more information.

CARPENTRY

CARPENTRY

As well as doing all general building carpentry work such as construction of timber building frameworks and structures including stairways, door frames with doors, and windows, Wake Construction Group Carpentry Division specialise in the application of CLT panels (Cross Laminated Timber) as a building material with excellent structural properties.

CLT is considered the engineered wood of the future, making the construction of entire buildings (floors, walls and roofs) from timber a reality - see Oval Hotel.

The potential of CLT as a sustainable building material is only just being realised around the globe, making Wake Construction Group a leader.

LININGS

LININGS

Wake Construction Group Linings Division understand the commercial and residential building demands put on wall and ceiling linings, of which, have a lasting impact on the comfort, amenity and performance of occupants.

Our skilled tradespeople employ rapid installation and plasterboard finishing techniques that can give you significant cost savings. We specialise in the construction of Shaft Wall Systems for fire rated performance, acoustic performance linings as well as decorative finish linings.

ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Wake Construction Group was established in 1980 by carpenter and builder Don Wake. From Don's legacy, Wake Construction Group has grown to become a leading South Australian COMMERCIAL and RESIDENTIAL building company.

We have more than four decades of experience in building excellence and currently employ more than 50 locally based staff, not to mention the jobs to hundreds more local subcontractors and suppliers.

Our current leadership team is Tony Cirillo General Manager, Matthew Wake Carpentry, Andrew Calderbank Linings, and Carolyn Paterson Administration.
