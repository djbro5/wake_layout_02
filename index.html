ABOUT US

ABOUT US

 The Wake Construction Group was established in 1980 by Don Wake. With a legacy built on consistently achieving results through hard work and fairness, the Wake Construction Group through the current leadership team, has grown to be a leading South Australian based commercial and residential Construction and Interiors company. We currently employ more than 50 locally based staff and proudly provide jobs to hundreds more local subcontractors and suppliers. Wake Construction Group is a respected construction company with more than four decades of experience in building excellence. We operate across the whole of South Australia.

WAKE CONSTRUCTION SYSTEM

WAKE CONSTRUCTION SYSTEM

 The Wake Construction System is our methodical approach with all projects. It is based on work methods learned from four decades of delivering quality buildings and creating client relationships. Simply put, our philosophy is to establish values of trust, integrity, honesty and professionalism and captures the many elements our dedicated team has identified as factors that play a vital role in each project’s success. These factors can be summed up as: • Solutions Focus – Every challenge can be overcome with creative thinking and a commitment to accomplishing the best possible outcome for the client and the construction team. • Teamwork – Working as a partner with the client, suppliers and our subcontractors.
