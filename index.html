ABOUT US

With a legacy built on consistently achieving results through hard work and fairness, Wake Construction Group through the current leadership team, has grown to be a leading South Australian based commercial and residential Construction and Interiors company. Today Wake Constructions employs 50+ local based staff, and proudly provides jobs to hundreds more local subcontractors and suppliers. Wake Construction Group is a respected construction company with more than four decades of experience in building excellence. Established by Don Wake in 1980, the company operates across South Australia.
